Work with us

Are you an independent carer in Essex?

Register with Essex Care Connect and we'll send you clients in your local area — no agency fees, no middleman. You set your own hours, your own rates, and work directly with families.

We match you with families who need exactly the kind of care you offer, in the area you cover.

🔒 DBS check required. To protect the families we work with, all independent carers must hold a valid enhanced DBS certificate. We'll ask for your DBS number when you apply. Don't have one yet? You can apply at gov.uk/request-copy-criminal-record.
💷

Keep more of what you earn

No agency taking 30–40% of your rate. Work directly with families and agree your own pay.

🗓️

Choose your own hours

You decide when you work. Morning visits, evenings, weekends, live-in — you set the terms.

📍

Work close to home

We only send you clients in your local Essex area — no long commutes to unfamiliar areas.

🆓

Free to register
